About the Artist

Danilo Reyes

Danilo Reyes is a multidisciplinary artist and craftsperson with roots in both music and visual arts. Known in the community as both a singer-gardener and innovative doll designer, Danilo brings a unique perspective to his hand-painted creations.

Each doll in the Dil Designs collection is a meditation on identity, artistry, and storytelling. Danilo's approach merges traditional craftsmanship with contemporary vision, creating pieces that are simultaneously nostalgic and forward-looking.

With a background spanning music, performance, and visual design, Danilo crafts each doll as a complete character—a figure with depth, history, and soul. These are not mere dolls; they are artistic statements.

Every piece is entirely hand-painted and tailored, making each doll truly one-of-a-kind. Limited editions ensure exclusivity and artistic integrity.
